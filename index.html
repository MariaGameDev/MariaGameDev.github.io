Music

I began playing violin at the age of six. During my second year at the Chernihiv Music College named after Revutsky, I joined the Chernihiv Philharmonic Orchestra, where I gained my first professional orchestral experience.

Alongside performing, I worked as a private violin teacher, helping young musicians develop technique, confidence, and musical thinking.

Outside classical music, I played violin in a Ukrainian rock band, performing at festivals and concerts across the country—an experience that shaped my rhythm, creativity, and improvisational style. Also, I played in indian musical band.

In 2022, I performed with the Golden Gate Symphony Orchestra (San Francisco), continuing my orchestral journey internationally.
